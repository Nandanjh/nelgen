About Nalgen Bio

Pioneering Biotechnology Solutions with Precision and Integrity

Our Mission

At Nalgen Bio, our mission is to accelerate scientific discovery and healthcare advancement by providing cutting-edge genomic and proteomic solutions with uncompromising quality and reliability.

We are committed to empowering researchers, healthcare professionals, and biotechnology innovators with the tools and services they need to address complex biological challenges and improve human health.

Our Vision

We envision a future where advanced biotechnology is accessible to all research institutions across India, enabling breakthrough discoveries that transform healthcare and improve lives.

Nalgen Bio aims to be the trusted partner of choice for life science research, known for our scientific expertise, innovative solutions, and unwavering commitment to customer success.

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Nalgen Bio emerged from a vision to bridge the gap in high-quality biotechnology services and products in India. Our founders, experienced scientists with backgrounds in genomics and molecular biology, recognized the need for reliable, locally-available research solutions that meet international standards.

Starting with a small laboratory focused on DNA sequencing services, we have steadily expanded our capabilities to encompass a comprehensive range of genomic, proteomic, and laboratory solutions. Today, Nalgen Bio serves hundreds of research institutions, pharmaceutical companies, and healthcare organizations across India.

Throughout our journey, we have remained true to our founding principles of scientific excellence, integrity, and customer-focused innovation. As we continue to grow, we remain committed to advancing the frontiers of biotechnology in India and contributing to the global scientific community.

Research and Innovation Initiatives
